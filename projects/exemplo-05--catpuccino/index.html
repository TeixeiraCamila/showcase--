What is a Cat Café?

A Unique Experience

A cat café is a space where you can enjoy delicious coffee while spending time with adorable rescue cats. It's the perfect blend of specialty coffee culture and feline companionship.

Founded in 2014, Catpuccino was one of the first cat cafés in Brazil. Our cats come from partner NGOs and streets, rescued from abandonment and urban situations. Every cat receives veterinary care, vaccination, and microchipping until they find their forever home.

The Experience

Safe & Relaxing Environment

Our Cat Lounge

Our cat lounge is completely separate from the café area, ensuring a calm and safe environment for both cats and visitors. The separation reduces stress for the cats, prevents food-related incidents, and maintains a hygienic space. We follow strict hygiene protocols and limit guest numbers to protect our feline residents' wellbeing.

15 Max Guests

House Rules

  • No flash photography
  • No picking up cats
  • Keep voices gentle
  • No outside food
  • Children under 12 must be supervised
  • Hands must be sanitized before entry
